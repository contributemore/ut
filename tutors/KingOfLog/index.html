King of Log

face sessions to a few of mathematics modules to university undergraduate students. This company is not limited to only university modules but can also accommodate high school students as well.


Payment amount is subject to the module weight and session duration or the overall

duration for the monthly services, and type of session it is. Payment must be made at

the end of every one-on-one session, or any time before the end of the overall sessions

for the monthly services. Any student is advised to have and share the proof of payment.
